For You

I’m writing this from miles away, from a place that doesn’t feel like home because you’re not here. Being overseas taught me something — distance doesn’t reduce love, it reveals it. I miss you in the quiet moments, in the loud ones, and in the spaces between. My cute little angryyyy bird… even if life never lets us walk together, you will still be my last two heartbeats until my very last breath.

— Lokesh Goud
